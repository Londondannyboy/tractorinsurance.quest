## Phone Contact

### Members Line

**Phone number:** 0345 606 6166

**Opening hours:**
- Monday to Friday: 8am to 6pm
- Closed weekends and bank holidays

<Callout type="tip" title="Best Times to Call">
Lines are busiest on Mondays and at month end. Try calling mid-week in the afternoon for shorter wait times.
</Callout>

### What You'll Need

When calling, have ready:

- Your Teacher Reference Number
- Your National Insurance Number
- Date of birth
- Recent payslip or pension statement

### International Calls

If calling from abroad: +44 345 606 6166

(Standard international rates apply)

> Mowden Hall
> Staindrop Road
> Darlington
> DL3 9EE

### When to Write

- Sending original documents
- Formal complaints
- Complex queries needing detailed explanation
- When you need a written response

## Employer Contact

If you're currently teaching, your employer can help with:

- Current contribution queries
- Pensionable pay questions
- Service records
- Scheme membership status

Contact your school's HR or payroll department first for these issues.

## Making a Complaint

If you're unhappy with the service:

### Step 1: Informal Resolution

Contact Teachers' Pensions first to try to resolve the issue.

### Step 2: Formal Complaint

Write to:

> **Complaints Team**
> Teachers' Pensions
> Mowden Hall
> Darlington
> DL3 9EE
