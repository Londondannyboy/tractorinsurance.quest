### 2015 Scheme Formula

For a rough estimate:

1. **Take your average career salary**
2. **Multiply by years of service**
3. **Divide by 54**
4. **Add approximately 30-50%** for revaluation (depending on career length)

## Boosting Your NHS Pension

If your pension is below average or below your expectations:

### Options to Increase Your Pension

1. **Work longer**: Each additional year adds more pension
2. **Seek promotion**: Higher salary = higher pension
3. **Increase hours**: If part-time, consider working more
4. **Buy Additional Pension**: Pay to boost your pension (voluntary)
5. **Delay retirement**: Avoid early retirement reductions
6. **Check for gaps**: Ensure all service is recorded correctly
