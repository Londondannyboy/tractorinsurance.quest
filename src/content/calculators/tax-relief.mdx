## Claiming Higher/Additional Rate Relief

### Automatic Relief (Everyone)

- 20% relief is added automatically by your pension provider
- No action needed
- Shows as the "gross" contribution

### Extra Relief (Higher Earners)

If you pay higher or additional rate tax:

1. **Via self-assessment**: Declare pension contributions on tax return
2. **Adjustment to tax code**: HMRC adjusts your tax code to give relief through PAYE
3. **Contact HMRC**: Can request adjustment if not doing self-assessment
