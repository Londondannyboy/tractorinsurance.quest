**My Civil Service Pension** is the online portal for managing your Civil Service Pension. This guide shows you how to login, register, and use the key features.

## How to Access the Portal

<ExternalLinkCard
  href="https://www.civilservicepensionscheme.org.uk/"

### Steps to Login

1. Go to civilservicepensionscheme.org.uk
2. Click "Member login" or "Pension Portal"
3. Enter your username and password
4. Complete any security verification
5. Access your pension dashboard

<Callout type="tip" title="Forgotten Login Details?">
Click "Forgotten password?" on the login page. You'll need your registered email address to reset.
</Callout>

2. Enter your pension number and National Insurance number
3. Verify your identity
4. Create a password
5. Login with your new credentials

## Your Pension Estimate

The estimate section shows:

- Your current pension value
- Projected pension at Normal Pension Age
- Years of pensionable service
- Breakdown by scheme (if you have benefits in multiple schemes)

<Callout type="info" title="Estimate Assumptions">
The estimate assumes you continue working until Normal Pension Age. Actual pension may differ if circumstances change.
</Callout>

## Annual Benefit Statement

Each year, a statement is produced showing:

- Your pension benefits as at 31 March
- Death benefits payable
- Your qualifying service
- Lump sum options

Access via: **Documents > Annual Benefit Statement**

## Nominating Beneficiaries

You can nominate who receives your death benefits:

### Death Grant

- Lump sum paid if you die
- Nominate anyone
- Can split between multiple people

### To Update Nominations

1. Login to the portal
2. Go to "Nominations" or "Expression of Wish"
3. Add or edit beneficiaries
4. Save your changes

<Callout type="warning" title="Keep Nominations Updated">
Review nominations after life events (marriage, divorce, children). Outdated nominations mean benefits may not go where intended.
</Callout>

## Retirement Modeller

The portal includes a modeller to help plan retirement:

- Model different retirement dates
- See impact of early/late retirement
- Compare lump sum options
- Understand reductions for early retirement

## Security Tips

### Protecting Your Account

1. **Use a strong password** - Mix letters, numbers, symbols
2. **Don't share login details** - Even with family
3. **Log out when finished** - Especially on shared devices
4. **Update email if it changes** - To receive important notices
5. **Check for https** - Ensure padlock icon shows

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Scams">
MyCSP will never email asking for your full password or bank details. Report suspicious emails.
</Callout>
