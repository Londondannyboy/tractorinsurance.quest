This applies each year. Your total pension is the sum of each year's pension, with revaluation applied.

### Step 2: Apply Revaluation

Each year's pension is increased by **CPI + 1.6%** at the start of every tax year.

### Step 3: Add Up All Years

Your total pension is the sum of all revalued years' pension.
