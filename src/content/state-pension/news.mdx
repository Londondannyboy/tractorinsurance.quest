## Triple Lock News

The government has confirmed the **triple lock** will continue for the duration of this Parliament:

<Callout type="info" title="Triple Lock Protected">
The Autumn Budget 2024 confirmed pensioner incomes will continue to be protected by the triple lock, ensuring the State Pension rises by the highest of earnings growth, inflation, or 2.5%.
</Callout>

## Winter Fuel Payment Changes

From winter 2024/25, Winter Fuel Payments are now **means-tested**:

### Who Still Qualifies

- Pension Credit claimants
- Those receiving certain other benefits
- People born before 23 September 1958 (for 2024/25)

<Callout type="warning" title="Check Your Eligibility">
If you're not receiving Pension Credit but have a low income, you may want to check if you qualify. Claiming Pension Credit could also restore your Winter Fuel Payment.
