### Step 2: Apply Revaluation

Each year's pension is increased by **CPI + 1.5%** at the start of every tax year until you retire.

<Callout type="example" title="Revaluation Example">
If you earned Â£556 pension in Year 1 and CPI is 2%:

- After 1 year: Â£556 Ã— 1.035 = Â£575
- After 2 years: Â£575 Ã— 1.035 = Â£595
- And so on...

This compounding significantly increases early-career pension value.
</Callout>

### Step 3: Add Up All Years

Your total pension is the sum of all revalued years' pension.
