## How to Use Our Calculators

### Step 1: Gather Information

Before using our calculators, collect:

- Your current pension pot values
- National Insurance record (for State Pension)
- Expected retirement age
- Current salary and contribution rates
- Any defined benefit pension statements

### Step 2: Run the Calculations

Use the relevant calculator(s) for your situation. Most people need multiple calculators to get the full picture.

### Step 3: Consider Your Options

The calculators show estimates - use them to:

- Decide if you're saving enough
- Compare retirement age scenarios
- Understand tax relief benefits
- Plan your income strategy

<Callout type="warning" title="Calculator Limitations">
Our calculators provide estimates based on the information you enter. Actual pension amounts depend on many factors and may differ from projections. Always check official sources and consider financial advice for major decisions.
</Callout>
