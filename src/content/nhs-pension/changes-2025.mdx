### What Changed?

The October 2024 changes introduced:

- **More tiers**: 11 contribution tiers instead of 7
- **Smoother progression**: Smaller jumps between bands
- **Lower rates for some**: Some mid-earners pay slightly less

<Callout type="info" title="Your Contribution Rate">
Your contribution rate is based on your actual pensionable pay, not your full-time equivalent. If you work part-time, your tier is based on your actual earnings.
</Callout>

### Who's Affected?

You're affected by McCloud if you:

- Were a member of the 1995 or 2008 scheme on **31 March 2012**
- Were not within 10 years of your Normal Pension Age on that date
- Have service in the **remedy period** (1 April 2015 - 31 March 2022)

### What Choices Do You Have?

For the remedy period, you'll choose whether to receive benefits under:

- **Legacy scheme** (1995 or 2008 section) - typically better for higher earners or those close to retirement
- **Reformed scheme** (2015 scheme) - may be better for career average earners

<Callout type="important" title="You'll Be Contacted">
NHS Pensions will contact affected members with their Remediable Service Statement and choice options. You don't need to do anything until you receive your statement.
</Callout>

## Lifetime Allowance Abolition

The **Lifetime Allowance** (LTA) was abolished from **6 April 2024**. This is a significant positive change for NHS staff with large pensions.

### What This Means

- No more LTA tax charges on pension benefits
- No limit on how much pension you can build up
- Previous LTA protections no longer needed (but keep records)

## Pension Revaluation 2025

Your NHS Pension benefits are revalued each year. For the **2015 scheme**, benefits increase by **CPI + 1.5%**.

### 2025 Revaluation

- September 2024 CPI: **1.7%**
- Revaluation rate: **3.2%** (1.7% + 1.5%)
- Applied to: All 2015 scheme accrued benefits from April 2025

<Callout type="info" title="How Revaluation Works">
Each year's pension you've built up is increased. This compounds over time, so earlier years get multiple revaluations before you retire.
</Callout>

### Pension Increase for Pensioners

If you're already receiving your NHS Pension, it increases each April by CPI:

- April 2025 increase: Based on September 2024 CPI of **1.7%**

## No Changes to Employer Contribution

The **employer contribution rate** remains at **23.7%** of pensionable pay. This hasn't changed and isn't expected to change in 2025.
